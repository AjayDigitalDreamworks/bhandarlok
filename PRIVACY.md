🔐 Privacy Policy
Effective Date: June 30, 2025

Your privacy is important to us. Here's how we handle your data:

1. Information We Collect
Personal Info: Email address and optional name if you log in.

Location: Used only to sort events near you.

Event Activity: We store which events you attend and your feedback.

We do not store passwords (authentication is managed by Firebase Auth).

2. How We Use Your Data
To show relevant nearby Bhandaras.

To personalize the app experience.

To improve app functionality through feedback analytics.

3. Data Sharing
We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties.

We may share event data (excluding personal info) for promotion.

4. Data Storage
All data is securely stored in Google Firebase servers.

You can request deletion of your data at any time.

5. Your Rights
You can access, update, or delete your information.

For any privacy concern, contact us at: bhandarlok@app.support
